Board Profile

Mr. Habibur Rahman

Chairman, BIFPCL Board

Mr. Habibur Rahman is a Senior Secretary of the Power Division, Ministry of Power, Energy & Mineral Resources, Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh. With over three decades of distinguished experience in the civil service, he has spearheaded numerous policy reforms in the regional energy sector.

As Chairman of BIFPCL, he provides strategic direction for the 1320MW Maitree Super Thermal Power Project at Rampal, ensuring its alignment with national energy security goals and stringent environmental standards.
