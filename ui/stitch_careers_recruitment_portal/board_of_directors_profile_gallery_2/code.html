Mr. Habibur Rahman

Chairman, BIFPCL

Mr. Habibur Rahman is a Senior Secretary of the Power Division, Ministry of Power, Energy & Mineral Resources, Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh. With over three decades of experience in the civil service, he has spearheaded numerous policy reforms in the energy sector.

As Chairman of BIFPCL, he provides strategic direction for the 1320MW Maitree Super Thermal Power Project at Rampal, ensuring its alignment with national energy security goals and environmental standards.

He holds an Advanced Degree in Economics and has participated in high-level diplomatic energy summits globally, fostering the energy partnership between Bangladesh and India.
